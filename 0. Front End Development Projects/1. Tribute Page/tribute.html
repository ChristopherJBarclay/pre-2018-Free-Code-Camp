This tribute page is dedicated to a great pioneer in heavy metal music.

The creator of death metal: Chuck Schuldiner.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Chuck was the founder, songwriter, lead guitarist, vocalist, and frontman of the metal band known simply as: Death.

Chuck helped pioneer new vocal techniques, including the "low growl" and "high pitched scream" sounds employed by many death metal bands today.

Schuldiner's growling vocals and amazing guitar solos, combined with the dark themes and heavy instrumentation of Death, started a new genre of music, later coined "death metal."

Chuck's music lives on in Death's recordings, and through the members of the official Death cover band, Death For All.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Here's a timeline of Chuck's life:

  • 1967 - Born on Long Island, New York.
  • 1976 - Chuck starts playing guitar at the age of 9, after the death of his older brother.
  • 1983 - Forms band Mantas, a precurser to Death, at the age of 16.
  • 1987 - Death releases their first album, Scream Bloody Gore.
  • 1991 - Death releases their breakthrough album, "Human," solidifying their trademark "technical progressive" sound.
  • 1993 - Death releases "Individual Thought Patterns." Schuldiner takes on controversial subject matter in his lyrics.
  • 1995 - Death releases "Symbolic" and continues down the path of progression.
  • 1998 - Death releases their final album, "The Sound of Perseverence." Chuck revamps his screaming vocals again.
  • 1999 - Chuck diagnosed with brainstem cancer. Undergoes chemo therapy and is pronounced cured.
  • 2000 - Undergoes surgery to remove the necrotized tumor from his neck.
  • 2001 - Chuck's cancer returns. The metal community bans together to help the Schuldiner family pay for medical bills. Chuck dies in December.

These musicians (plus others) donated to help Chuck:

Dave Grohl, Ozzy Ozbourne, King Diamond, Napalm Death, Anthrax, Kid Rock, Korn, Red Hot Chili Peppers.



RIP Chuck Schuldiner!
